Our Account Types

New Verified Account

A fresh, verified Google Play Console account, ready for your first app. This is a personal account, ideal for individual developers starting their journey on the Google Play Store.

  • Verification: Fully verified and ready for app submission.
  • Age: Newly created.
  • History: No prior app history.

Old Verified Account with App

An established, verified account with a pre-existing app, offering higher trust. This type of account is perfect for developers looking for an account with a proven track record.

  • Verification: Fully verified.
  • Age: Varies, but generally over a year old.
  • History: Includes at least one published app, which can positively impact your new app's visibility.

Old Verified Account without App

An aged, verified account, perfect for a clean start with a history. This account provides the benefits of an established account without the baggage of existing apps.

  • Verification: Fully verified.
  • Age: Varies, but generally over a year old.
  • History: No prior app history, giving you a blank slate.

New Organizational Account

A new, verified organizational account, available with or without apps, for your business. This is a business account, which allows for team member access and more.

  • Verification: Fully verified for your organization.
  • Age: Newly created.
  • History: Available with or without pre-existing apps.
